About Us

 

We were born and raised at the base of the Cottonwood Canyons and have made these canyons our home. As active outdoor enthusiasts we wanted to create events where others could enjoy these beautiful mountains…this is how Vigor Utah was born.

FIRST THINGS FIRST

Our goal at Vigor Utah, is America and Utah first. It is our intention to have 100% of all products provided at Vigor events to be made locally. This includes: sponsors, shirts manufactured, medals manufactured (by Dynabuckle), local businesses supporting the finish line, etc. This may keep our product (bling) smaller, but please keep in mind it cost us more to bring Utah’s own products to you. Our hope is you would prefer to support our local economy with a smaller finisher medal, than support other nations by sporting the biggest medal out there. Either way, Vigor Utah appreciates your support!

Global Endurance is the parent company of Run Vigor.
