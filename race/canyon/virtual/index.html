Big Cottonwood Canyon Virtual

If you can't make it out to Run Vigor this year but you would still love to be a part of the Vigor family and you want to receive the awesome 2025 Run Vigor medal? Register for the Vigor Big Cottonwood Canyon Virtual Race and on May 10, 2025 simply walk/run a 5k, 10k or half marathon distance at the venue of your choice or on the treadmill.

Email Vigor at race@triutah.com with your distance and time and we will mail out your 2025 Vigor medal!
(Don't forget to post your pics or selfies on our facebook page)

Fees

$25 *Price does not include shirt, online registration fee or taxes.

Note: There is no race day registration. Registration will be open online till 6:00pm on 05/08/2025.
